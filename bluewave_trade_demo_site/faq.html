Frequently asked questions

Answers about how this demo behaves and what you can safely expect.

Is this a real trading platform?

No. This is a design demo only. All trading actions, prices, and balances are fake.

Are prices and charts real?

No. Charts and prices are static placeholders, reused across asset pages.

Does registration create an account?

No. The registration form is disabled and does not send data anywhere.

Can I connect my wallet or bank card?

No. There are no integrations in this demo. Wallet and payment flows are intentionally absent.

Why is everything blue?

The main design requirement was a calm, blue-themed interface. All key elements follow that palette.

Is my data stored or tracked?

In this static version, no personal information is collected or stored.

Can this UI be extended with a backend later?

Yes. The layout is prepared so you can plug in APIs, authentication, and database logic if needed.

Is any of this financial advice?

No. Nothing on this site should be considered investment advice.
